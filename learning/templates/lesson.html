Lesson: Solar Basics

Solar energy is derived from sunlight and is one of the most abundant renewable energy sources available to us. In Kenya, we are blessed with abundant sunshine throughout the year, making solar energy an ideal solution for our energy needs.

Key Concepts

Solar Energy

Energy harnessed from the sun's radiation, converted into electricity or heat.

Solar Panels

Devices that convert sunlight directly into electricity using photovoltaic cells.

Cost Savings

Solar systems reduce electricity bills and provide long-term energy savings.

Environmental Benefits

Clean energy source that reduces carbon emissions and environmental impact.
